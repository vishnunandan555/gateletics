Frequently Asked Questions

Is my study data kept private?

Yes. By default, GATEletics operates in Offline Mode. All your checklist progress and session logs are stored strictly on your local device. If you optionally choose to sign in with Google, backups are securely encrypted and synchronized to your private Firebase database.

Does the app support offline usage?

Absolutely. All core features (syllabus checklist, progress statistics, and focus timers) work completely offline. Internet is only required if you choose to sync your data across devices.

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your subject completion tracker and erase local database cache from the Settings screen inside the app at any time.

How do I delete my cloud synced account?

You can delete your account instantly from the settings menu within the app, or you can submit an email request. Please see our Account Deletion Page for complete details.
